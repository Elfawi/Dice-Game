Overview of the Game

The game involves two players taking turns. On each turn:

  • A player rolls a dice.
  • The result of the dice is added to the player's current score.
  • player can either hold or roll again.
  • If they hold, the current score is added to the total score, and the turn passes to the next player.
  • If they roll a 1, they lose all points from the current turn, and the turn passes to the other player.
  • The first player to reach 100 points wins the game.
